In line with the transformation process of the country initiated in 1998 by the Indonesian reformation movement to become one of the largest democratic countries in the world; the legal needs of both domestic and foreign business in recent years have been tremendously increased in complexity. In addition, the frequency of domestic, global transactions which require legal advice and broad experience in various practices areas is also increasing. Businesses are now more cautious of legal risks that may arise from their decision based on the existing laws and regulations which become more complex. In order to meet the needs of our clients in such a condition, the ability of our law firm consisting of teams of lawyers possessing both knowledge and experience in the various legal specialty areas necessary to deal with our clients’ need is paramountly important.

We are continuously endeavoring to develop the expertise of our firm’s members through various training and ongoing education arrangement and at the same time sharing our member’s collective knowledge experience, skills and databases. We require all firm members to comply with and demonstrate the highest standards of professional ethics and integrity in their practice for providing legal services to our clients.
